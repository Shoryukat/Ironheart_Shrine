About Ironheart

Hailing from Chicago, Riri Williams is a 15 year old engineering student, who managed to design her own suit of armor. She's a certified super genius, who decides to use her talents to become a Superhero!

  1. She's a certified super genius.
  2. She is a skilled inventor.
  3. Her suit grants her superstrength
  4. Supersonic flight
  5. Has Energy repulsor and missle projection
